About Us

I Can Institute of Commerce, Nanded

" I started a new Venture in the form of professional coaching classes for the students wishing to make their career in ‘COMMERCE’. "

As the Planned location is in the heart of Nanded, (Above Bangalore bakery, Vazirabad) so it becomes very much necessary to know the interest of the students as well as conduct one or two weeks demo batch in order to create interest of students in our coaching classes.

If the students are satisfied in demo batch than it will be very much easy to launch new batch that to with a huge response. In my opinion attracting students is the main task before starting such type of Profession. The coaching class will include following classes and subjects: (English and Marathi medium both)

  • 11th Commerce: - Book-keeping and accountancy, Economics, Secretarial Practice, Organization of commerce and management , English
  • 12th commerce: - Book-keeping and accountancy, Economics, Secretarial Practice, Organization of commerce and management, English.
  • Bcom 1st year, 2nd year, 3rd year only account subjects( Practical subjects statistics,taxation, etc)


There are two semesters in each year in Bcom . Bcom 1st Year = 1st semester & 2nd Semester Bcom 2nd year = 3rd semester & 4th semester Bcom 3rd year = 5th semester & 6th semester

Students can also opt for a single semester and a single subject.
